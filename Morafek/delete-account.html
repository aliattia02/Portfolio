Delete Your Account

CareMate, developed by Clida.Tech (Ali Attia)

This page explains how users of the CareMate app can request deletion of their account and associated data, in line with Google Play's account deletion requirements.

How to Request Deletion

  1. In‑app (recommended): Open CareMate → go to Profile → Settings → Account → tap Delete Account → confirm the request. Your request will be processed within 30 days.
  2. By email: If you no longer have app access, send a deletion request from your registered email address to contact@clidatech.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", including your registered email and, if known, your patient/clinician account ID.
  3. Verification: We may ask you to verify your identity before processing the request, to prevent unauthorized deletion of your data.

What Gets Deleted

Data typeAction
Account credentials & profile (name, email, role)Deleted
Vitals & observations (HR, SpO₂, glucose, BP)Deleted
Encounters, conditions & diagnosis codesDeleted
Uploaded documents (Cloudinary)Deleted
Messages / communication threadsDeleted
Authentication tokens (device keystore)Revoked & deleted immediately

What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period even after account deletion:

  • Records required by German healthcare record‑keeping obligations may be retained for up to 10 years, as required by applicable medical documentation law, even though your account access is removed.
  • Anonymized or aggregated data (no longer linked to you) may be retained for analytics and service improvement.
  • Security and fraud‑prevention logs (e.g. IP address, timestamps) may be retained for up to 90 days.
  • Backup copies are purged on a rolling cycle and may persist for up to 30 days after deletion before being fully overwritten.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and associated data (other than data required to be retained as described above) have been deleted.
